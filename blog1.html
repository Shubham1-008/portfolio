Importance of Sense Organs

We humans are given with five sense organs in our body that organs are (Eyes,Ear,Skin,Mouth,Nose).These senses seems to be very normal but in facts they are the mightes thing that a human being posssess. In Bhagwat Geeta Krishna Emphasises over the fact that one should learn or practice to control his senses with his will. Krshna says that the senses should work according to a man but the thing is that the man is the slave of his sense . He keeps on satisfying his senses and then gradually he tends to loose his grip over his mentak state n start becoming unstable. So everyone should know how to control over the senses and to conquer them to have a stable mind and lifestyle.

The negative emotions like Anger, Jealousy,Stres and Anxiouesness should be controlled or hey can ruin you.
